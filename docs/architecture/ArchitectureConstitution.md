# SafetyMAIN Architecture Constitution

Version: 1.0

Status: Approved

Date: 2026-07-07

---

# Preamble

SafetyMAIN is an AI-native Enterprise Compliance Operating System.

Its purpose is to transform enterprise knowledge, regulatory requirements and compliance obligations into a unified digital platform.

The architecture exists to ensure consistency, explainability, extensibility and long-term sustainability.

This Constitution defines the immutable principles governing the evolution of the SafetyMAIN platform.

---

# Article I — Knowledge First

Enterprise knowledge is the primary asset of the platform.

Knowledge is represented through Knowledge Objects.

All other artifacts originate from knowledge.

---

# Article II — Documents Are Evidence

Documents are not the source of truth.

Documents are generated representations of enterprise knowledge.

Evidence confirms fulfillment of obligations.

---

# Article III — Metadata Describes the Business

Business variability belongs to metadata.

Platform variability belongs to software.

Whenever possible, business customization shall occur through metadata.

---

# Article IV — Rules Describe Behavior

Business behavior is defined through Compliance Rules.

Business workflows shall not be embedded directly in application code.

---

# Article V — Obligations Drive Compliance

The platform manages obligations rather than documents.

Every obligation has an owner, a lifecycle and verifiable evidence.

---

# Article VI — AI Is Native

Artificial Intelligence is an intrinsic capability of the platform.

AI assists but does not replace human responsibility.

AI recommendations should be explainable whenever possible.

---

# Article VII — Version Everything

Every meaningful change shall be versioned.

Historical knowledge is part of enterprise knowledge.

---

# Article VIII — Organization Is the Boundary

Every Knowledge Object belongs to an Organization.

Organization defines ownership, security and responsibility.

---

# Article IX — One Concept, One Term

The Domain Dictionary defines official terminology.

The same concept shall not have multiple competing names.

---

# Article X — Architecture Before Implementation

Implementation follows architecture.

Architecture follows understanding.

No implementation shall violate this Constitution.

---

# Closing Statement

Technology evolves.

Programming languages change.

Frameworks become obsolete.

Enterprise knowledge endures.

SafetyMAIN is built to preserve, connect and operationalize that knowledge for decades.
